Gas & Safety Sensors

Gas and safety sensors are used to detect harmful gases, smoke, and unsafe environmental conditions in IoT projects.

MQ2 – Gas Sensor

What it is: Detects LPG, smoke, hydrogen, and methane gases.

Working: Gas concentration changes the sensor resistance, producing an analog output.

Used in:

  • Gas leakage detection
  • Home safety alarms
  • Industrial gas monitoring (basic)

MQ7 – Carbon Monoxide Sensor

What it is: Detects carbon monoxide (CO) gas.

Working: Requires heating cycles to accurately sense CO levels.

Used in:

  • CO leakage alarms
  • Vehicle emission monitoring (demo)

MQ135 – Air Quality Sensor

What it is: Measures air quality including NH3, NOx, alcohol, benzene.

Working: Gas exposure alters conductivity of the sensor material.

Used in:

  • Air quality monitoring
  • Smart city demo projects

Smoke Sensor

What it is: Detects smoke particles in air.

Working: Uses optical or ionization principles to sense smoke.

Used in:

  • Fire alarm systems
  • Home safety alerts

Flame Sensor

What it is: Detects infrared light emitted by flames.

Working: Flame IR radiation triggers the sensor output.

Used in:

  • Fire detection systems
  • Industrial safety demos

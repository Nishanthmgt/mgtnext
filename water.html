Water & Level Sensors

Water and level sensors are used to detect the presence, level, or flow of water in IoT and embedded system projects.

Water Level Sensor

What it is: Detects the presence and level of water.

Working: Water contact changes conductivity between sensor traces.

Used in:

  • Water tank level monitoring
  • Overflow alert systems
  • Smart irrigation demos

Soil Moisture Sensor

What it is: Measures moisture content in soil.

Working: Soil conductivity varies with moisture level.

Used in:

  • Automatic plant watering
  • Smart agriculture projects

Rain Sensor

What it is: Detects rainfall or water droplets.

Working: Water droplets change resistance on sensor surface.

Used in:

  • Rain alert systems
  • Weather monitoring

Water Flow Sensor

What it is: Measures the flow rate of water.

Working: Internal rotor generates pulses proportional to flow.

Used in:

  • Water usage monitoring
  • Smart water management

Float Switch

What it is: Mechanical switch activated by liquid level.

Working: Float movement opens or closes the switch.

Used in:

  • Tank level control
  • Pump automation systems

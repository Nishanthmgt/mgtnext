Environmental Sensors

Environmental sensors are used to measure temperature, humidity, pressure, and air quality. These sensors are widely used in IoT, smart cities, weather monitoring, and automation projects.

DHT11

Temperature & Humidity Sensor

  • Digital output
  • Low cost
  • Beginner friendly
Used in basic weather stations, room monitoring, and IoT learning projects.

DHT22

High Accuracy Temp & Humidity Sensor

  • Higher accuracy than DHT11
  • Wider temperature range
Used in smart agriculture and indoor climate monitoring.

LM35

Analog Temperature Sensor

  • Analog output
  • Easy calibration
Used in industrial temperature measurement and embedded systems.

BMP280

Temperature & Pressure Sensor

  • I2C / SPI communication
  • High precision
Used in altitude measurement, drones, and weather monitoring.

MQ135

Air Quality Sensor

  • Detects CO₂, NH₃, smoke
  • Analog output
Used in pollution monitoring and smart city applications.

BME280

Temp, Humidity & Pressure

  • All-in-one sensor
  • I2C communication
Used in advanced IoT and environmental analytics systems.
